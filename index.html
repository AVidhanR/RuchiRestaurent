Naan

Naan is a soft and fluffy Indian-style bread made from white flour. It's baked in a tandoor, or clay oven, which gives it a distinctive charred and smoky flavor. Perfect for dipping into our rich and flavorful curries.

Price: $10.99

Aloo Gobi

Aloo Gobi is a classic Indian dish made from potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi). It's cooked with a blend of spices, including turmeric, cumin, and coriander, which give it a vibrant yellow color and a warm, earthy flavor. This dish is vegetarian-friendly and pairs well with both rice and naan.

Price: $8.99

Pakora

Pakora is a popular Indian snack or appetizer, made by dipping vegetables like onion, potato, spinach, or paneer in a seasoned chickpea flour batter and then deep-frying until golden and crispy. It's often served with a side of tangy tamarind chutney or cool mint yogurt sauce for dipping.

Price: $12.99
